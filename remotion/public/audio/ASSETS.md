## CC0 SFX Library (`cc0-sfx/`)

A curated collection of sound effects for video production:

### Impacts & Hits
- `hit_01.ogg` - `hit_04.ogg` - General impact sounds
- `sfx100v2_hit_01.ogg` - `sfx100v2_hit_03.ogg` - Additional hits
- `slam_01.ogg` - `slam_07.ogg` - Heavy slam/impact sounds
- `metal_01.ogg` - `metal_12.ogg` - Metallic impacts
- `sfx100v2_metal_hit_01.ogg` - `sfx100v2_metal_hit_02.ogg` - Metal hits

### Transitions & Whooshes
- `swish-1.wav` - `swish-13.wav` - Various swish/whoosh sounds (light to heavy)

### Dramatic Effects
- `explosion.ogg` - Explosion sound
- `gong_01.ogg`, `gong_02.ogg` - Gong/cymbal hits
- `sfx100v2_thunder_01.ogg` - Thunder/boom effect

## Usage Notes

1. **For Video Production**: Use `poseidon-beat.wav` as main BGM, sync to 120 BPM
2. **Transitions**: `swish-*.wav` for scene cuts (light: 1-4, heavy: 5-13)
3. **Logo/Title Reveals**: `gong_*.ogg` or `sfx100v2_thunder_01.ogg`
4. **UI Interactions**: `hit_*.ogg` or `metal_*.ogg`
5. **Dramatic Moments**: `slam_*.ogg` or `explosion.ogg`
